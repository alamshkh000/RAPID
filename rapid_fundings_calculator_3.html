Reseller Activation Progression Incentives for Distributors (RAPID) AWS Objectives 2 During this presentation, you will learn: 1 How AWS is increasing investment into the AWS Distribution Program 2 The enhancements to RAPID 3 Overview of RAPID and benefits 4 Call to Action Introduction 3 Amazon Web Services (AWS) is enhancing support for partners looking to resell AWS services and expand their business models through AWS Distributors. We’re investing in scaling partner support via Distributors to better equip them with enablement, go-to-market resources, and training—boosting AWS engagement and expanding customer reach through AWS Marketplace. RAPID The number of partners engaged with AWS Distributors has doubled YoY (from 3,200 in January 2023 to 6,700+ till date). The number of customers that Distributors are selling to has also grown exponentially, doubling YoY. Our data shows that partners working with authorized AWS Distributors see up to 114% YoY increase in revenue, and 392% increase in opportunity launches. Partners can reduce their time-to-first-sale by up to 50%, from an average of 7 months to 3 months. To help new and existing partners capitalize on this opportunity and expand their customer footprint, AWS is investing in its Distribution channel as a key route to market for both commercial and public sector partners. 4 5 AWS stands out in the industry by providing a comprehensive business development framework through its Distribution channel, which is uniquely designed to accelerate partner capabilities, reduce time-to-market, and drive mutual business outcomes. RAPID in 2025 6 RAPID is a sector-agnostic program that integrates the two previously separate programs, and: Includes financial incentives throughout the partner lifecycle Rewards authorized distributors for both development of strategic partners, and activation of new transacting partners and MRR growth Provides a clear roadmap of activities, performance milestones and financial incentives Results in decreased effort for Distributors through AWS structured guidance and automated processes Leverages a proven, repeatable framework to develop all AWS Partners Current State Future State Two separate programs: RAPID Program for all Partners DLAD Program for Public Sector Partners RAPID in 2025 7 Beginning January 1, 2025, there will be enhancements to RAPID: Program integration and simplification: Distribution-Led Accelerated Development (DLAD) will integrate with RAPID. RAPID will expand to include three motions: RAPID GTM Ready (formerly DLAD; for authorized AWS Distributors only) RAPID Activate (formerly Reseller Growth Motion) RAPID Grow (formerly RGM Accelerators) RAPID GTM Ready will be available to all Distribution Sellers (Commercial Sector and Public Sector). Note: there may be additional considerations for AWS Distributors with RAPID GTM Ready in a Strategic Collaboration Agreement (SCA) Distributor Benefits 8 In 2025, we’re enhancing and streamlining RAPID for AWS Distributors to include three motions: RAPID GTM Ready (formerly DLAD) RAPID Activate (formerly Reseller Growth Motion) RAPID Grow (formerly RGM Accelerators) The maximum a distributor can earn across all three motions is $63k per Distributor Seller, which consists of: $30k maximum incentive for RAPID GTM Ready + $25k maximum for RAPID Activate + $8k for RAPID Grow* = $63k total per distribution seller *For RAPID Grow, the maximum incentive is $8k because the distributor would have already been paid for the PSF listing during the RAPID GTM Ready motion RAPID GTM Ready (formerly DLAD) 9 Distributors will lead partners through the AWS Partner Development Framework.
